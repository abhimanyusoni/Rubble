Introduction

Rubble is an enterprise Project Management Platform built for leading Property and Construction Management Company of United Kingdom. Goal was to custom design and develop a platform which provides people and process management across multiple degrees to manage construction process execution. It’s all about using technology to make the execution more efficient and effective.

CMARIX has designed and developed user-friendly task management tools to manage team work easier and enhance productivity. Its global customers use PHP and YII 2.0 Framework for a robust and secure platform.
